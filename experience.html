my joyful experience


The most unforgettable experience for me is when my friends and I finished to memorize Juzuk 1 Al-Quran.

To appreciate our efforts, our school held the program "Majlis Pensijilan Tahfiz Al-Barakah"

I remember that time we so happy to receive the certificate from school.

Memorizing Al-Quran makes me realize that we have to read Al-Quran enthusiastically and carefully to avoid any errors.



experience that i want to go through


It was always a dream of mine, and one shared by millions of other Muslims, to go perform Hajj and Umrah at Mecca.

Mecca is a holy place of Muslims in Saudi Arabia where it is believed that the prophet Muhammad lived and taught more than 1,300 years ago.

Mecca is the holiest city in Islam. There are billions of people coming to Mecca for Hajj from all over the world.

I hope one day that I wil become one of the billions too. I want to look with my eyes about how beautiful Mecca is.
